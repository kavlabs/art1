ABOUT NASMEEN

Nasmeen Kousar is an artist based in Chennai, India. She believes that beauty can best be expressed through art. Drawing inspiration from nature, her work spans the sea, the sky, as well as flowers and orchards. Palette knives, sculpture, and texture paste with acrylics are the primary tools she uses in her technique to create 3D art pieces, delicately crafted on canvas and wooden boards, each piece is the result of endless hours of dedication to capturing the mesmerizing details of the painting.
